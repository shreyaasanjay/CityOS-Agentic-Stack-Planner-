# Research Paper Writing — 3 Agents

Three researchers are co-authoring a paper with 3 sections (intro, methods, results). Each section can only be edited by one researcher at a time.

## Agents
- **RESEARCHER_A**: Writes the connection between introduction and methods. Needs to edit **INTRO_SECTION** and **METHODS_SECTION**.
- **RESEARCHER_B**: Writes the connection between methods and results. Needs to edit **METHODS_SECTION** and **RESULTS_SECTION**.
- **RESEARCHER_C**: Writes the comparative analysis between results and introduction. Needs to edit **RESULTS_SECTION** and **INTRO_SECTION**.

## Shared Resources
- **INTRO_SECTION**: Introduction section of the shared document
- **METHODS_SECTION**: Methods section of the shared document
- **RESULTS_SECTION**: Results section of the shared document

## Workflow

Each researcher first reads literature and drafts content locally. When ready to write into the shared document, they write their content into the required sections, then review their own work locally — if the review finds issues, the researcher reports the finding and finishes.

## Goal
All three researchers complete their literature review, drafting, writing, and review phases without conflicts over shared document sections.
