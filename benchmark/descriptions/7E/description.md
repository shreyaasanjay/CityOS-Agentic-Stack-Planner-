# Task 7E: Two-Author Shared Document

Two writers are co-authoring a **DOCUMENT**. Each writer is primarily responsible for one chapter, but either writer may need to edit the other's chapter to maintain cross-chapter consistency. An **EDITOR** reviews the complete **DOCUMENT** and may request revisions to any chapter.

## Agents

### WRITER_A
Primarily writes chapter 1 (background and methodology), may edit chapter 2 for consistency.

### WRITER_B
Primarily writes chapter 2 (results and analysis), may edit chapter 1 for consistency.

### EDITOR
Reviews both chapters together, ensures consistency, requests revisions if needed.

## Shared Resources

- **DOCUMENT**: shared document for collaborative writing

## Workflow

1. **WRITER_A** and **WRITER_B** write their respective chapters concurrently.
2. Either writer may need to edit the other's chapter for cross-chapter consistency.
3. Writers update the references in **DOCUMENT** to add citations for their sections.
4. Once both writers have finished writing and updating references for their chapters, the **EDITOR** begins review.
5. If the **EDITOR** finds inconsistencies, the relevant writer must revise. The **EDITOR** may need to re-review after revisions.
6. The **EDITOR** combines the final sections into the completed **DOCUMENT**.

## Goal

All writers complete their chapters, the editor reviews and approves the document, and the final document is assembled.
